<h2>About Dataset</h2>

This dataset contains simulated global BMW vehicle sales data enriched with key market, economic, and operational factors across multiple countries and years. It is designed for data analysis, visualization, and machine learning tasks related to automotive sales performance.

The dataset includes information on sales volume, vehicle models, segments, engine types, pricing, marketing spend, dealership presence, fuel prices, and macro-economic indicators such as GDP growth and interest rates. It enables users to explore sales trends, regional performance, electric vs. fuel vehicle adoption, and the impact of market conditions on sales.

<h2>This dataset is well-suited for:</h2>

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Regression and forecasting models

Time-series trend analysis

Business and market insights

Kaggle practice and portfolio projects

<h2>Note:</h2>
This dataset is synthetically generated for educational and analytical purposes and does not represent official BMW sales figures
